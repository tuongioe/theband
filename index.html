THE BLACK PINK

Let's kill this love

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of four members: Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Debuting in 2016, the group quickly rose to global stardom with their powerful performances, catchy music, and unique style. Known for hits like "DDU-DU DDU-DU", "Kill This Love", and "How You Like That", BLACKPINK has become a cultural phenomenon, breaking numerous records and performing on some of the world’s biggest stages. With a strong international fanbase, they are not only music icons but also influential fashion and beauty trendsetters.
